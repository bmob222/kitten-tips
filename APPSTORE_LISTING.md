### Description
Your complete guide to cat care — from bringing home a new kitten to caring for a senior cat.

Kitten Tips gives you daily expert advice, a behavior decoder, age-by-age milestones, and an adorable animated kitten companion named Whiskers who lives right on your screen.

FEATURES:
- Daily rotating cat care tip — a new one every day
- 40+ tips across 10 categories: Health, Feeding, Safety, Behavior, Grooming, Training, Bonding, and more
- Kitten Age Guide — milestones from newborn (0-2 weeks) through senior (7+ years)
- Behavior Decoder — what does my cat mean? Decode 16 common cat behaviors like slow blinking, kneading, and tail language
- Virtual Kitten — meet Whiskers! He walks, sleeps, eats, and hangs out on your screen. Tap to pet, feed, or let him nap
- Save your favorite tips with the heart button
- Beautiful, clean design that cat lovers will adore

Whether you're a first-time kitten parent or a lifelong cat person, Kitten Tips has something for you. Download free and give your cat the best care possible.
