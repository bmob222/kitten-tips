Need help? Here are answers to common questions.

FAQ

How do I save a tip?

Tap the heart icon on any tip to save it. Find your saved tips in the "Saved" tab.

How does the daily tip work?

A new tip is shown each day, rotating through our collection of 40+ expert cat care tips. Check back daily for fresh advice!

Can I interact with the kitten?

Yes! Tap Whiskers (the animated kitten) to pet him. You can also feed him, give him a nap, or just watch him explore your screen.

Why do I see ads?

Kitten Tips is free! Ads help us keep it that way. We use Google AdMob for non-intrusive banner ads.

Is my data private?

Yes. All your saved tips and preferences stay on your device. We don't collect or store any personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.
